Assured discount on groups of 25 people or more

Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo offers group discounts for groups of 25 people or more (entry only).

So why not suggest a visit for your next staff function, play group or friends gathering?

Guided Tours

We offer exclusive guided tours for large or small groups. At an additional cost to entry, you can book a guided tour for 1.5 hours to help you explore the wonders of the deep. Advanced bookings essential and subject to availability.

Special Needs Groups

For special needs groups, we are able to offer a substantial discount on Entry ticket prices. Advanced Bookings essential, with company/group letter required.
